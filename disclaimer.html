Educational Purpose

Our resources are designed for general learning for children aged 2–6 years. All content — including blog posts, printable worksheets, activities, and learning games — is created with the goal of supporting early childhood education in a fun and engaging way.

Every child's learning journey is unique. We encourage parents and teachers to adapt our resources to each child's individual pace, learning style, and readiness level. Our materials are guides, not rigid programs.

No Guarantees

While we strive to provide high-quality, research-informed educational content, we do not guarantee specific learning outcomes. A child's progress depends on many factors including their individual development, engagement, practice, and home environment.

Little Genius Kids makes no claims that use of our content will result in any specific academic achievement or developmental milestone. Results vary from child to child and depend heavily on consistent, supportive parental or teacher engagement.

Important: If you have developmental concerns about your child, please consult a qualified paediatrician, educational psychologist, or specialist. Our content is not a substitute for professional advice.

Supervision

Parents and teachers are responsible for supervising children while using this website. We strongly recommend sitting with your child during interactive sessions, especially for younger children under 4 years of age.

We are not responsible for any unsupervised use of our platform by children. Adults should review all activities and content before sharing with children to ensure suitability for their specific needs.
