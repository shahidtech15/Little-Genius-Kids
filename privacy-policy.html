🔒 Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Last Updated: March 17, 2026
At Little Genius Kids, we take privacy seriously — especially because our audience includes young children and their families. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our website:

  • Personal information — such as your name and email address, only when voluntarily submitted.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type, and general geographic location.
  • Cookies — small text files placed on your device to improve your experience.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our website is designed for parents, teachers, and caregivers.

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve and maintain our website.
  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Deliver a better user experience (e.g., remembering theme preferences).
  • Display relevant advertisements through AdSense.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to analyze site traffic and remember your settings. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect some site features.

Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your Google account settings.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This website complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.
