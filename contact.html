Frequently Asked Questions

Is Little Genius Kids free to use?

Yes! All our core educational activities, games, worksheets, and videos are completely free. We may display some curated, child-safe advertisements to keep the service running.

Is the content safe for my child?

Absolutely. We review all content for age-appropriateness. Our platform does not include social features, external links from activities, or data collection from children under 13.

Can teachers use this platform for their classrooms?

Yes! Our worksheets and activities are perfect for classroom use. Teachers can download and print our PDF worksheets and use our interactive games on classroom displays.

How can I suggest a new topic or activity?

We love new ideas! Use the form above with subject "Content Request" and describe what you'd like to see. We often build requests from parents and teachers!
