🔒 Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Last Updated: March 17, 2026
At Little Genius Kids, we take privacy seriously — especially because our audience includes young children and their families. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit littlegeniuskids.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our website:

  • Personal information — such as your name and email address, only when voluntarily submitted through our contact form
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on pages, browser type, device, and general geographic location (country/region)
  • Cookies and tracking data — small text files placed on your device to improve your experience and enable advertising (see Cookies section below)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our website is designed for parents, teachers, and caregivers — not for direct use by young children unsupervised.

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To improve and maintain our website — understanding which content is popular helps us serve you better
  • To respond to your inquiries — when you contact us via the form or email, we use the information you provide to reply
  • To deliver a better user experience — remembering preferences such as dark mode, and ensuring the site loads correctly on your device
  • To display relevant advertisements — through third-party ad services including Google AdSense (see below)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to:

  • Remember your site preferences (e.g. dark mode setting)
  • Analyse how visitors use the site via analytics tools
  • Serve relevant advertisements through third-party networks

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect some features of the site.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads based on your visits to sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Advertising Privacy Policy.

Google AdSense Disclosure

We may display advertisements on this website served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.

  • Google AdSense uses the DART cookie, which allows it to serve ads based on browsing history
  • Users may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings
  • Third-party ad servers or ad networks may also use cookies to measure advertising effectiveness
  • Little Genius Kids has no access to or control over the cookies used by third-party advertisers

We are committed to displaying only family-friendly, child-safe advertisements on this platform.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Our website may contain links to other websites. Our Privacy Policy applies only to littlegeniuskids.com. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This website is committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at shahidali.shahid@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Your Consent

By using the Little Genius Kids website, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
